Tips

  • Write clear, unambiguous questions with a single correct answer
  • Make all distractors (wrong answers) plausible
  • Keep options similar in length and format
  • Avoid "All of the above" or "None of the above"
  • Questions are randomized for each student session

All questions are stored in MongoDB and immediately available to students
