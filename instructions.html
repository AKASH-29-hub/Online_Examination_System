Exam Instructions

General Rules

  • The exam consists of 10 multiple-choice questions
  • You have 30 minutes to complete the exam
  • Questions are randomized each session
  • Each question has one correct answer
  • You can navigate between questions freely
  • The exam auto-submits when time runs out

Anti-Cheating Policy

  • Do NOT switch tabs — this will trigger a warning
  • Do NOT exit fullscreen mode during the exam
  • Webcam monitoring is active throughout
  • All keyboard and mouse activity is logged
  • 3 warnings = auto-submission of your exam
  • All violations are recorded and reviewed
